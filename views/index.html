Our Vision

To be a leader in Pakistan's maritime industry by ensuring 100% compliance with the Hong Kong Convention (HKC) for the safe and environmentally sound recycling of ships We strive to set the highest standards in worker health and safety while promoting sustainable practices and responsible dismantling of end of life vessels to protect our oceans and environment.

Our Mission

To deliver ship recycling services that prioritize environmental responsibility, worker safety, and regulatory compliance, while continuously improving our methods through innovation and global best practices to support a cleaner, safer, and more sustainable maritime industry.

About Us | Salam's International Green Ship Recycling

Salam's International Green Ship Recycling is a leading industrial ship recycling firm specializing in marine safety and environmental sustainability. Our yard is located in Karachi, offering a wide range of services including dismantling, recycling, and disposal of end-of-life vessels.

We are committed to providing high-quality services that adhere to international standards and contribute to a cleaner, safer, and more sustainable maritime industry.
