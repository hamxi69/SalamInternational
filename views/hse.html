HSE MANAGEMENT

Our HSE Commitments

Emergency Response

Our yard is equipped with a dedicated Emergency Response Team (ERT) We conduct regular fire drills confined space rescue simulations and oil spill containment exercises to ensure immediate and effective action in any critical situation.

Risk Assessment

Comprehensive risk analysis is conducted for every vessel covering hot work heavy lifting and hazardous material handling We ensure all potential hazards are identified and mitigated before a single torch is lit.

Environmental Protection

We follow strict protocols for the safe removal and disposal of asbestos PCBs and heavy metals Our commitment ensures zero discharge into the sea keeping the Gadani coastline clean and protected.

Ship Recycling Plan (SRP)

We develop a custom SRP for every vessel before arrival. This includes a detailed Inventory of Hazardous Materials (IHM) and a step-by-step dismantling strategy to ensure safety and environmental protection.
