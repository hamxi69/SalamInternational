ABOUT & HISTORY

About Salam’s International

Salam’s International is a leading industrial ship recycling firm specializing in maritime safety and environmental sustainability. Our yard is ClassNK Certified, and our mission is to recycle end-of-life vessels using safe and modern techniques, setting new benchmarks for the industry.

History

With years of experience in the maritime sector, our company has always prioritized safety and quality. Since our inception, we have successfully executed complex projects, positioning ourselves at the pinnacle of the ship recycling industry today.

MILESTONES
2018 – Pioneer in Standards

We became the first ISO-certified company in our sector, laying the foundation for our quality management.

2023 – Global Recognition

The IMO Secretary-General, Mr. Arsenio Dominguez, visited our yard, validating our superior safety and security standards.

2026 – ClassNK Excellence

We achieved our first ClassNK Certification, underlining our technical expertise and operational excellence.

Top HKC Ranking

Today, our yard is proudly ranked 2nd in HKC (Hong Kong Convention) compliance.

CERTIFICATIONS
ClassNK Certification

Our yard is certified by Nippon Kaiji Kyokai, a globally recognized authority in ship recycling.

Hong Kong Convention (HKC)

We strictly follow HKC guidelines to ensure environmentally sound and safe ship recycling.

Operational Excellence

We believe in practical, on-ground safety and technical precision beyond just paperwork.
