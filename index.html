What We Do

Ship Dismantling

Environmentally sound and regulation-compliant ship recycling ensures the safe dismantling of vessels, minimizing ecological impact while adhering to international laws and safety standards. This sustainable approach promotes responsible waste management, pollution prevention, and resource recovery from end-of-life ships.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan’s most trusted ship recycling name by pioneering the shift toward ClassNK compliance, sustainable operations, and innovation-driven practices that ensure environmental safety, worker welfare, and responsible dismantling of end-of-life vessels.

Our Mission

To deliver ship recycling services that prioritize environmental responsibility, worker safety, and regulatory compliance, while continuously improving our methods through innovation and global best practices to support a cleaner, safer, and more sustainable maritime industry.

Who We Are

Salam’s International is a leading industrial ship recycling firm specializing in maritime safety and environmental sustainability. Our yard is ClassNK Certified, and our mission is to recycle end-of-life vessels using safe and modern techniques, setting new benchmarks for the industry.

With over two decades of operational excellence, we are dedicated to supporting the country’s iron and steel needs through sustainable and environmentally sound practices.
