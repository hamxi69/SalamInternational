Salam's International is not just a dismantling facility—we are architects of sustainable maritime resource recovery.

Founded in 2000, our journey has been defined by a relentless pursuit of safety and environmental integrity. Today, our Gadani facility stands as a beacon of HKC compliance, transforming retired giants of the sea into the raw materials of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To lead the global maritime industry towards a completely circular, zero-waste lifecycle.

Our Mission

Delivering world-class engineering solutions that protect our oceans and our workforce.
