THE VISIONARIES

Meet Our
Leadership

The experts driving sustainable innovation in global ship recycling.

Rafiqe Salam

Cheif Executive Officer

Rafiqe Salam is the Founder and CEO of Salam’s International and a pioneering leader in Pakistan’s ship recycling industry. With over 30 years of experience, he guided the company to become the country’s first ClassNK (HKC) certified yard. Committed to environmental sustainability, worker safety, and global compliance, he continues to drive innovation and ethical practices, making Salam’s International a trusted partner for safe and environmentally responsible ship recycling.

Abdul Haseeb

Chief Operating Officer

Abdul Haseeb is the Chief Operating Officer of Salam’s International, bringing a modern and innovative approach to operations. He bridges traditional industry expertise with international maritime standards, leading digital and operational improvements while strengthening global compliance. Committed to sustainability and efficiency, he is driving the company toward a greener and more progressive future.

Sartaj Lala

Ship Supervisor

Sartaj Lala is the backbone of our ship-breaking operations With decades of expertise in the art of ship dismantling he oversees the most complex stages of structural breakdown and precision cutting at our yard His technical mastery and leadership ensure that every project is executed with clinical efficiency strictly adhering to international safety and environmental standards.

Qari Fazal Subhan Usmani

Production Manager

Qari Fazal Subhan Usmani is a cornerstone of our production operations With extensive experience in ship recycling and industrial production he manages all yard operations with strategic precision His mission is to maintain peak operational efficiency while striking an ideal balance between quality and safety ensuring every project is completed on schedule and in full compliance with international standards

Muhammad Ismail Khan

HSE Manager

Muhammad Ismail Khan leads the Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) department at our organization As Manager, he is responsible for designing the yard’s safety framework and ensuring 100% compliance with international maritime regulations including HKC and ClassNK standards His extensive experience and vision have positioned our company as a leader in industrial safety where he oversees rigorous risk management and safety audits to turn our 'Zero Accident' policy into a reality.

Salam Rehman

HSE Officer

Salam Rehman plays a vital role in maintaining our Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) standards. As a dedicated HSE Officer, he is responsible for the practical implementation of safety protocols, conducting daily site inspections, and leading Hazard Identification (HAZID) processes. Salam ensures that all yard activities align with international maritime regulations, focusing on worker training and accident prevention to maintain our high safety benchmarks.

Ahsan Ali

Field Supervisor

Ahsan Ali manages loading and logistics operations at the yard with the utmost responsibility With specialized expertise in heavy machinery and material handling, Ahsan ensures the safe and efficient loading of scrap and recycled materials Under his dedicated supervision, the yard’s transport workflow remains highly organized, resulting in significant time savings and optimized operational output.

Ch Muhammad Afzal

Firstaider

Ch Muhammad Afzal is responsible for medical safety and emergency response within our team In the challenging environment of a ship recycling yard his top priority is ensuring the health and emergency medical care of the workers An expert in first aid protocols and emergency handling, Afzal remains on-site at all times to provide immediate and professional medical assistance in the event of any safety issue or injury.
